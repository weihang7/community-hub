FAQ

How does it work?

It’s simple. Login with Google or Facebook—don’t worry, we won't’ post anything without your permission—and enter your zip code as well as what you're looking for, whether that be a service to render or a service to receive.

What if I’m an organization? How can I find people through your service?

Login with Google or Facebook and fill out our opportunities form; Simply list a who, what, where and why, and you’ll be off to attracting volunteers.

Why should I volunteer through your system?

Why shouldn’t you? Volunteering is fun, and using our matching system will only help you. Not only will you get certified volunteer hours by Community Hub, you’ll also earn Community points for each the service you do. With Community points comes the chance for you to be on the board of the website!

How does this tool differ from others?

The biggest difference between us and all the other services is we’ll help you find exactly what you want. With our custom made software, we’ll match your needs of frequency, location and interest with those of people in need. We’ll also stand by you as you volunteer, and make sure you’re recognized for your work.
